## Synopsis
The pilgrims enter a land where dreams and reality blur. A demon feeds on fear, trapping victims in nightmares that feel all too real. Only by confronting their deepest anxieties can the pilgrims escape.

## Teaching
*"Fear is a dream we mistake for reality. Courage wakes us to truth."*

## Synopsis
After a demon disguised as a teacher humiliates Pigsy for his ignorance, Pigsy vows to learn wisdom. His journey to enlightenment is predictably chaotic, but surprisingly earnest.

## Teaching
*"Learning begins with humility. The fool who seeks wisdom is wiser than the sage who believes he knows all."*

## Pigsy Nonsense Rating
🐷🐷🐷🐷 — Pigsy's attempts at scholarship are hilarious and heartfelt.

## Commentary
A rare episode where Pigsy is the hero of his own story. His journey from ignorance to effort, even if the result is imperfect, is unexpectedly touching.

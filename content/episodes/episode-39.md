## Synopsis
The pilgrims discover a legendary fountain that promises eternal youth. But the demon guarding it reveals that immortality without purpose is a curse. Pigsy's desire to drink nearly traps them all in eternal stagnation.

## Teaching
*"Youth is wasted on the young, and immortality on the foolish. Life's meaning lies in its fleeting nature."*

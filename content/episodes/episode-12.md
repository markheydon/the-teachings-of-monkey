## Synopsis
Pigsy is lured by a demon offering magical pearls, only to discover they're cursed. His greed nearly costs the pilgrims their lives as the pearls drain their energy. Monkey must break the enchantment before it's too late.

## Teaching
*"Treasure offered freely is rarely free. True wealth lies in what cannot be stolen."*

## Pigsy Nonsense Rating
🐷🐷🐷🐷🐷 — Peak Pigsy chaos: greed weaponised into pure disaster.

## Commentary
The episode's title is a playful nod to the proverb, and Pigsy embodies it perfectly. His inability to resist shiny objects creates comedic mayhem while delivering a sharp lesson about desire and deception.

## Synopsis
Demons create perfect duplicates of the pilgrims to discredit their quest and spread chaos. Villages turn against Tripitaka when his "double" commits terrible acts. The real pilgrims must prove their identity through deeds, not words.

## Teaching
*"Reputation is easily tarnished but slowly rebuilt. Actions speak louder than accusations."*

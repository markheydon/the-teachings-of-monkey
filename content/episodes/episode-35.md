## Synopsis
The pilgrims find themselves in a liminal space between celestial and infernal realms. A demon judge forces them to account for their actions, weighing their virtues against their sins. Only honesty can tip the scales.

## Teaching
*"Judgment is not imposed from above, but earned through action. We are the sum of our choices."*

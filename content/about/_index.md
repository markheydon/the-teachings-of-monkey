Born from an egg on a mountain top, this archive began as a nostalgic spark — a reminder of *Monkey* suddenly appearing again on Pluto TV in late 2025. For many of us who grew up in the 80s, the colours, the fighting, the chaotic camp were irresistible. Beneath the madness, though, there was always a thread of genuine wisdom.

This site is:
- A consolidation of the **teachings** from all 52 episodes.  
- A celebration of Pigsy’s nonsense, Tripitaka’s smackdowns, and demons of the week.  
- A mix of canonical lessons and AI‑generated riffs — because, as the theme song says, *“He knew every magic trick under the sun.”*

---

## Why It Exists
- To make the teachings accessible, readable, and fun.  
- To show how AI can help build and maintain a content‑heavy archive.  
- To invite remixing, contribution, and playful participation — because Monkey’s nature is irrepressible.

## A Little Irrepressible Spirit
Just like the Stone Monkey, this project was born from an egg on a mountain top. With a staff of truth and a cloud to ride, it carries forward the spirit of the punkiest monkey that ever popped.  

Whether you’re here for enlightenment, nostalgia, or just Pigsy’s magic trousers, we hope you enjoy the journey as much as we enjoyed building it.

Monkey’s impulsiveness often clashes with Tripitaka’s restraint. These episodes highlight the importance of discipline in the face of chaos.

---

## Key Episodes
- **Episode 1: Monkey Goes Wild About Heaven**  
  *Teaching:* Pride without discipline leads to downfall.  
- **Episode 15: Pigsy’s Magic Tummy**  
  *Teaching:* Appetite must be tempered by wisdom.  
- **Episode 23: Two Little Blessings**  
  *Teaching:* Desire without foresight becomes burden.

---

Self‑discipline is the thread that keeps the pilgrims moving forward, even when temptation beckons.

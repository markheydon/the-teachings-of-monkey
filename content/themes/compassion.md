Compassion is the antidote to illusion and deception. These episodes remind us to see beyond appearances.

---

## Key Episodes
- **Episode 2: Monkey Turns Nursemaid**  
  *Teaching:* Compassion tempers chaos.  
- **Episode 8: Pigsy Woos a Widow**  
  *Teaching:* Lust blinds judgment, but compassion restores balance.  
- **Episode 19: The Demon Bride**  
  *Teaching:* True compassion resists false seduction.

---

Compassion is the pilgrims’ greatest weapon — stronger than Monkey’s staff or Sandy’s strength.

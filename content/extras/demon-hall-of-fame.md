Every week, the pilgrims face a new demon — some terrifying, some ridiculous, all memorable. Here are the standouts who earned a place in the Hall of Fame.

---

## 1. **[The Dog Demon (Episode 9)](/episodes/episode-09/)**
The Dog Demon uses disguise and deception to snatch a village girl, but is ultimately undone by Monkey's cunning and Pigsy's appetite.

---

## 2. **[The Well Demon (Episode 10)](/episodes/episode-10/)**
An aquatic ambusher who turns a simple well into a watery trap. Nearly makes Pigsy the centrepiece of an underwater banquet.

---

## 3. **[The Day-Night Demon (Episode 11)](/episodes/episode-11/)**
A celestial trickster who controls day and night, plunging villages into eternal darkness. Undone by Monkey's wit.

---

## 4. **[The Pearl Demon (Episode 12)](/episodes/episode-12/)**
Offers cursed magical pearls that drain victims' life force. Exploits Pigsy's greed to near-fatal effect.

---

## 5. **[The Minx and Slug Duo (Episode 13)](/episodes/episode-13/)**
A seductive minx paired with a grotesque slug companion. The contrast between glamour and slime is peak Monkey weirdness.

---

## 6. **[The Catfish Saint (Episode 14)](/episodes/episode-14/)**
A catfish demon disguised as a holy saint. Proof that even holiness can be mimicked by demons.

---

## 7. **[The Demon Digger (Episode 15)](/episodes/episode-15/)**
A tunnel-dwelling demon who traps the pilgrims in an underground labyrinth. Tests their unity and resourcefulness.

---

## 8. **[The Most Monstrous Monster (Episode 16)](/episodes/episode-16/)**
A demon whose terrifying appearance conceals unexpected complexity. Challenges the pilgrims to look beyond surface horror.

---

## 9. **[The Grey Gloves Devil (Episode 17)](/episodes/episode-17/)**
A master manipulator who wields truth itself as a weapon, turning allies against each other through carefully crafted lies.

---

## 10. **[The Locust King (Episode 18)](/episodes/episode-18/)**
Commands a plague of demonic locusts that devour entire villages. Embodies the destructive power of unchecked greed.

---

## 11. **[The Vampire Master (Episode 19)](/episodes/episode-19/)**
Drains life force rather than blood, blending Eastern and Western demon lore into gothic horror. Defeated by serene resolve.

---

## 12. **[Wun-Lung and the Three Witches (Episode 22)](/episodes/episode-22/)**
The undead demon and his witch allies create a trap powered by belief itself. A genuinely eerie supernatural threat.

---

## 13. **[The Jealousy Demon (Episode 24)](/episodes/episode-24/)**
A demon disguised as a beautiful woman who stokes jealousy and discord. Turns communities against themselves with whispered suspicions.

---

## 14. **[The Nightmare Demon (Episode 25)](/episodes/episode-25/)**
Feeds on fear by trapping victims in nightmares that blur with reality. A genuinely eerie villain who weaponises anxiety.

---

## 15. **[The False Buddha (Episode 26)](/episodes/episode-26/)**
Impersonates enlightenment itself, offering false shortcuts to wisdom. Tests whether the pilgrims truly understand their quest.

---

## 16. **[The Dogs of Death (Episode 28)](/episodes/episode-28/)**
A pack of demonic hounds that hunt those who show fear. They prove that terror can be conquered only through courage.

---

## 17. **[The Gambler Demon (Episode 29)](/episodes/episode-29/)**
Challenges victims to life-or-death games where pride becomes the real enemy. Monkey's arrogance nearly costs them everything.

---

## 18. **[The Vengeful Spirit (Episode 32)](/episodes/episode-32/)**
A ghost who feeds on guilt and regret, forcing each pilgrim to confront their past. Gothic horror meets emotional depth.

---

## 19. **[The Dream Demon (Episode 33)](/episodes/episode-33/)**
Creates nested layers of illusion where reality becomes impossible to discern. A mind-bending challenge to consciousness itself.

---

## 20. **[The Torment Demon (Episode 34)](/episodes/episode-34/)**
Curses an emperor with visions of his own cruelty. Proves that power without compassion creates its own punishment.

---

## 21. **[The Identity Thief (Episode 37)](/episodes/episode-37/)**
Steals the pilgrims' identities, creating perfect duplicates that force existential questions. Who are you when faced with yourself?

---

## 22. **[The Shadow Demon (Episode 41)](/episodes/episode-41/)**
Casts an enormous shadow that grows with each victory, feeding on pride and triumph. A brilliant metaphor made manifest.

---

## 23. **[The Dancing Demon (Episode 42)](/episodes/episode-42/)**
Enchants victims with irresistible music, forcing them to dance until exhaustion. Camp chaos with a sinister edge.

---

## 24. **[The Karma Demon (Episode 43)](/episodes/episode-43/)**
Creates a village where generosity and selfishness are instantly rewarded or punished, testing the pilgrims' true motives.

---

## 25. **[The Polite Monster (Episode 44)](/episodes/episode-44/)**
A demon of perfect manners who slowly drains life force through hospitality. Evil served with impeccable etiquette.

---

## 26. **[The Painting Demon (Episode 46)](/episodes/episode-46/)**
Traps souls inside beautiful paintings, creating a gallery of frozen victims. Art becomes imprisonment.

---

## 27. **[The Nostalgia Demon (Episode 47)](/episodes/episode-47/)**
Feeds on grief and memory by creating visions of the pilgrims' mothers, trapping them in false reunions.

---

## 28. **[The Petrifying Demon (Episode 49)](/episodes/episode-49/)**
Turns victims to stone with a glance, creating a haunting garden of petrified travellers. A truly chilling villain.

---

## 29. **[The Wolf Demon (Episode 50)](/episodes/episode-50/)**
Exploits hunger during a famine, offering food in exchange for souls. Desperation becomes the weapon.

---

## 30. **[The False Emperor (Episode 51)](/episodes/episode-51/)**
Claims dominion over heaven and earth, wielding seemingly absolute authority until questioned about true leadership.

---

The Hall of Fame celebrates the creativity of *Monkey's* villains — equal parts menace and camp comedy.
